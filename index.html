Chris Richardson

Chris is an attorney based in Ballito, assisting clients on the North Coast and greater Durban area. He has a wealth of experience in a wide variety of legal disciplines with a particular focus on corporate, commercial, construction, litigation and labour law.

Chris matriculated at Kearsney College and obtained his BA LLB at the University of Natal, Pietermaritzburg. He went on to complete his articles with a large, reputable Durban based law firm. In 2005 he was admitted as an attorney.
​
His experience in the commercial sector has given him the unique ability to blend the practice of law with a strong corporate understanding. With a genuine desire for solutions-orientated outcomes, he identifies his clients’ requirements and applies the law with a pragmatic approach. Chris is currently a consultant at C. Ries Incorporated.
​
Chris is married with three children. Spare time is family time, although as Chairman of the Salt Rock Squash Club, he certainly enjoys playing a regular game of squash, with the occasional round of golf thrown in.
